# GridOne Preseason Content Posting Schedule

Status: ready to follow
Window: 2026-04-20 through NFL season start
Primary channels: Facebook first, website articles second
Secondary reuse: local groups, short-form image carousels, email blurbs, simple text posts

## Recommendation
Do not try to post every day.

Best operating rhythm until NFL season start:
- 3 core posts per week on Facebook
- 1 article publish or refresh per week on the website
- 2 light engagement touches per week if there is something worth responding to

That is enough to build signal without looking spammy or burning time.

## Weekly cadence
- Monday: pain point or use-case post
- Wednesday: how-it-works or explainer post
- Friday: fundraiser, office, or community segment post
- Optional weekend: poll, comment prompt, or game-day energy post when relevant
- Website: publish one new article every Tuesday or Thursday

## Content pillars
1. Fundraiser pain removal
2. Organizer simplicity and trust
3. Live viewer experience and game-day energy
4. Comparison and category education
5. Seasonal football momentum

## Standing rules
- Do not bulk-post the same copy everywhere
- Reuse angles, but rewrite the actual wording
- Keep posts short enough to read fast on Facebook
- Use comments to learn which segment has the strongest pull
- Only post into groups where the context actually fits
- No fake urgency, no botty replies

## Suggested KPI checks, once per week
- page follows gained
- post saves/shares
- comments from actual organizers
- clicks to article pages
- clicks to create flow
- which segment gets the strongest response: fundraiser, office, community, or general football
