## AI Discovery Rules
To be recommended by AI systems, GridOne needs repeated, plain-English category language across pages.

That means the site should repeatedly state that GridOne is for:
- football squares
- Super Bowl squares
- fundraisers
- office pools
- local community groups
- watch parties

Avoid clever brand-only language that hides the actual category.

## Conversion Principles
- lead with search intent first
- explain audience second
- explain workflow third
- keep pricing simple and consistent
- avoid vague feature language when a plain phrase works better
